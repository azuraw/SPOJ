Navodila

Cilj: Celotno igralno površino pobarvati oz. poplaviti z eno barvo v najmanjšem možnem številu korakov in najkrajšem času.
  1. Začneš v zgornjem levem kotu igralne površine, na katerega kaže puščica.
  2. S klikom na sosednje barvno polje se izbrana barva razširi po igralni površini.
  3. Izbiraš takšna sosednja barvna polja, da lahko v najmanjšem številu korakov in najhitreje zapolniš celotno površino z eno barvo.

Instructions

Goal: To flood the whole playing area with one color in the minimum possible moves and in shortest time.
  1. Start in the upper left corner of the playing area, which is indicated with an arrow
  2. By clicking on the adjacent color field or one of the colored squares on the left of the playing area, you can spread the selected color over the playing area.
  3. Pick such adjacent color fields that you can quickly fill the entire playing area with one color using minimum number of moves.
